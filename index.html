4 best air purifying plants for the home

Pothos

Pothos is an evergreen plant. It has heart-shaped leaves with splashes of yellow. This plant is commonly used as a hanging plant in a house. It can climb through aerial roots, and wild or cultivated plants grown outdoors can reach tremendous heights using tall trees as support.

2. Snake

It improves air quality by absorbing toxins through its leaves and producing pure oxygen. It is an ideal bedroom plant. Most other plants discharge carbon dioxide at night, whereas the snake plants continue to produce oxygen.

3. Monstera Deliciosa

Monstera Deliciosa plants are native to Central America. It is famous for its large perforated leaves and its natural leaf-holes. It Thrives in bright to medium indirect light, and intense sunlight is not so preferable to this plant.

4. Chinese Evergreen

This green indoor plant discharges Oxygen, increasing efficiency and improving well-being and prosperity both in living and working spaces. Also, in China, this plant is considered a lucky plant.
